Registration & Fees

01

Fee Information

  • Registration Fee: £60.00 (non-refundable)
  • Deposit: 1 week fees payable before settling-in
  • Fees payable monthly in advance by Direct Debit, Standing Order, Cheque, or Cash
  • Sibling discount available for full-time attendees
  • Fees revised annually
02

Government Funding

  • 15 hours/week free Government Childcare Funding for eligible 3 and 4-year-olds after the term of their 3rd birthday
  • Applied by Nursery, reflected in fees
  • Sibling Discount applies for full-time attendance (not on free funding)
