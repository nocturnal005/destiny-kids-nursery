### Hero

### Our Story
- Founded in 2011 with a single mission: provide quality childcare
- Expanded to two locations: Battersea and Brixton Hill
- Ofsted regulated with consistent positive reviews
- Serve the local community across Lambeth and Wandsworth

### Mission & Values
- **Mission:** "To provide uppermost quality and skilled childcare, meeting every child's emotional, physical, cultural, and educational needs in a stimulating, safe, and loving environment."

  1. **Learning Through Play** — Children learn best when they enjoy the process

### Fee Information
- Registration fee: £60 (non-refundable)
- Payment methods: Direct Debit, Standing Order, Cheque, Cash
- Monthly calculation: (Weekly fee × 52) ÷ 12
- Sibling discount available for full-time attendees
- One month written notice required for withdrawal
