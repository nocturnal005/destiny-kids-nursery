Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

🕐 What are your opening hours?

Both branches are open Monday to Friday, 7:30am to 6:30pm, 51 weeks of the year (closed on public holidays and between Christmas and New Year).

👶 What ages do you accept?

We welcome children from 3 months to 4 years old across three dedicated rooms: Babies, Toddlers, and Pre-School.

🎟️ How do I book a tour?

Use 'Book a Tour' in the contact form above, call either branch, or email us — we offer tours on weekdays at flexible times.

💷 Do you accept government funding?

Yes! We accept 15 and 30 hours free childcare, childcare vouchers, and tax-free childcare.

🍎 Are meals and snacks included?

All meals and healthy snacks are freshly prepared on-site and included in our fees. We cater for dietary requirements and allergies.
