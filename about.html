Our Story

A Legacy of Learning

2011

Founded

Destiny Kids Nursery founded in Battersea with a heartfelt mission: to create a place where every child feels safe, valued, and inspired to learn.

2013

Ofsted Registered

Achieved Ofsted registration and successfully completed our first inspection — a testament to the quality of our early years care and education.

2015

Online Presence

Launched our first website and online presence, connecting with more local families and sharing our story with the community.

2018

Expanded Services

Expanded our services and programmes, updating our policies to meet GDPR compliance while enriching the learning experience for every child.

2020

Brixton Hill Branch

Opened our second branch at 228 Brixton Hill, serving families across Lambeth with the same warmth and dedication.

2024

13 Years of Care

Celebrating 13 years of nurturing care, having supported hundreds of children and families on their early years journey.

2026

New Chapter

Continuing to grow, invest, and innovate new pathways to create value for children and their families.

M&V

Mission & Values

To provide the highest quality childcare that meets every child's emotional, physical, cultural, and educational needs — in a stimulating, safe, and loving environment where families feel genuinely welcomed and supported.

01

Learning Through Play

Children learn best when they're having fun. Every activity sparks curiosity, encourages exploration, and builds skills through the joy of play.

02

Welcoming & Inclusive

We celebrate diversity, respect differences, and create an environment where every child and family belongs.

03

Partnership with Parents

We see ourselves as an extension of your family. Through open communication and regular progress reports, we keep you connected every step of the way.

04

Quality & Excellence

Our staff undergo continuous professional development, our curriculum evolves with best practice, and we hold ourselves to the highest standards.
